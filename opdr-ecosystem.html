Opdracht Ecosystems

1) What is an ecosystem for businesses?

A business ecosystem is the network of organizations—including suppliers, distributors, customers, competitors, government agencies, and so on—involved in the delivery of a specific product or service through both competition and cooperation.

2) Find out which customers are part of the Salesforce ecosystem.

all customers, partners, developers etc etc

3) What value does the Salesforce ecosystem provide to Salesforce customers?

kennis, innovatie, implementatie, klaar voor de toekomst, toegankelijk, gelijkwaardig,succes

4) What is a community for businesses?

groep van bedrijven die samenwerken om zo lokale gemeenschap te steunen.

5) Find out what you can do as a customer in the Salesforce community.

The customer serves as a place where customers can not only connect and answer each other’s questions, but also give us feedback on what we can do better. They can also suggest new prodyct features. leeromgeving apps installeren, trainingen volgen, certificeren, webinar etc etc

6) What is Idea Exchange in the Salesforce community?

the IdeaExchange is where you can share ideas with the Trailblazer Community and Salesforce product managers. With continuous voting and special prioritization cycles, you get to vote on ideas that matter most to you.
